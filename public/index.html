SUIT is Wolverhampton's award-winning Lived Experience Recovery Organisation, entirely run by people with first-hand experience of addiction and recovery. Established February 2007. A project of WVCA.

Three Simple Steps

There's no red tape here. Just real human support.

1

Walk In or Reach Out

Come to Paycare House on George Street or call us on 01902 328983. Drop-in Monday & Friday 10:00-15:00. No referral needed.

2

Meet Our Team

Every member of staff has lived experience of addiction and recovery. We listen, we understand, and we're here for you.

3

Build Your Recovery

Access wraparound support including advocacy, SMART groups, creative arts, buddy support, and one-to-one mentoring.

We Can Help You With…

No jargon. No hoops to jump through. Just support that works.

Peer Mentoring & Buddy Support

Regular check-in calls, guidance, care plans, and someone to listen. Our Buddy Support service pairs you with a peer mentor who truly understands.

Advocacy & Wraparound Support

Help with housing and homelessness, benefits (PIP/UC/ESA/JSA), form filling, debt advice, job searches, CV writing, and signposting to partner organisations.

Drop-In Sanctuary

A safe, warm place to be. Monday & Friday 10:00-15:00. Free tea, coffee, and real support from people who've been there. No appointment needed.

Creative Arts & Recovery

Art therapy, drama projects with Geese Theatre, spoken word, music sessions, Wolverhampton Literature Festival participation, and community exhibitions.
