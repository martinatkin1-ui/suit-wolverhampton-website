About the SUIT Family

SUIT (Service User Involvement Team) is Wolverhampton's Lived Experience Recovery Organisation (LERO), providing wraparound support for people struggling with, or in recovery from drugs & alcohol. SUIT are a project of WVCA (Wolverhampton Voluntary & Community Action) and work closely with Wolverhampton's treatment provider Recovery Near You. Established February 2007.

Our Mission

We believe that vulnerable people with substance use problems should be treated fairly, have access to services that focus on recovery, and should be able to engage with service providers that will support holistic measures of prevention, treatment and rehabilitation. SUIT provides advocacy and support for people experiencing stigma and complex vulnerabilities from challenges surrounding substance use.

Our Values

  • Lived Experience First — every staff member and volunteer has first-hand experience of addiction and recovery
  • Queen's Award for Voluntary Services (2014) — 'training recovering addicts and empowering those that are suffering from addiction'
  • European Model of Good Practice — recognised by the Street Support Project (EU) 2019 and BeTrAd (EU) 2018
  • College of Lived Experience (CLERO) Member — connecting with other lived experience organisations nationally
  • Creative Recovery — arts, drama, spoken word, and community projects as pathways to healing

Awards & Recognition

  • Queen's Award for Voluntary Services (2014)
  • European Model of Good Practice — Street Support Project EU (2019)
  • BeTrAd EU Recognition (2018)
  • CLERO Member — College of Lived Experience
